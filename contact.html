Research Collaboration

I'm open to collaborating on research projects related to my areas of interest, including:

  • Distributional policy analysis
  • Welfare measures and subjective well-being
  • Social preferences and distributive justice
  • Health economics and health behaviors

If you're working on a project in these areas and are looking for a collaborator, please reach out with details about your research. I'm particularly interested in interdisciplinary approaches that combine economic methods with insights from psychology, sociology, and public health.
